Speakers

The people students hear from, and what they work on. This is the planned roster; guests are confirmed closer to each date.

Nathaniel Hafer, PhD
Point-of-care diagnostics at UMass Chan; helped run the NIH RADx Tech program for rapid COVID testing. Opens the term on the innovation landscape.
Allan Jacobson, PhD
Distinguished Professor at UMass Chan and co-founder of PTC Therapeutics; his readthrough research underpins the drug ataluren. A bench-to-biotech case study.
