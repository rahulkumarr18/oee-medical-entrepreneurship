The region

Who does what, and where

We are based at UMass Chan in Worcester and open across Massachusetts, with events in the Boston and Cambridge area and in Worcester. We sit inside one of the country's densest medical-innovation corridors, around Mass General, Harvard Medical School, and hospitals across Massachusetts. The map below is practical: who can teach, who can build, who can fund, and who should hear the next pitch.

People to follow up with

The reason to build this network is simple. A medical idea gets better when the right people see it early: a physician who knows the workflow, an engineer who can prototype, a professor who can sharpen the science, and a founder who knows what breaks first.

That mix is hard to assemble from scratch. The incubator makes it normal to ask, present, follow up, and try the next version.
