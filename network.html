The region

Who does what, and where

UMass Chan sits in Worcester, about an hour west of one of the busiest healthcare-innovation corridors in the country. The elective helps students understand who does what, where ideas go, and who to talk to next.

UMass Chan at the center, the Boston and Cambridge corridor to the right, and a few national affiliations to the left. Drag your cursor across the map to move through it.

People to follow up with

The reason to spend six evenings on this is simple. You finish with a working picture of the region and a handful of people you can actually email, because you met them over dinner rather than through a cold introduction.

That is hard to put together from scratch during residency, and easy to start now, while a room of founders and investors is showing up specifically to meet students.
