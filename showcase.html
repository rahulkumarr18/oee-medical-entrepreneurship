Student showcase

Your idea, in front of the room

The elective is not only about listening. Students who want to can bring a clinical observation, sketch a concept, and present it to founders, investors, and peers. Nothing here is graded on whether it becomes a company.

How it works

Over the term, anyone who wants to can develop a single idea into a short pitch: the problem you noticed, who has it, and the roughest sketch of what you would do about it. You get feedback from the speakers and from the group as you go.

At the networking sessions, those pitches go in front of the wider room. The goal is not a polished deck. It is a first real conversation with people who can tell you whether the idea holds, and who to talk to next.
