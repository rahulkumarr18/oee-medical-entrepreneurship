UMass Chan Medical School · Open across Massachusetts

A medical entrepreneurship community for Massachusetts.

We help students, physicians, faculty, engineers, and founders understand how a clinical idea becomes a device, a diagnostic, or a company, and we connect them across the state's medical community. We meet over dinner in Boston and Worcester: talks from people who have built healthcare ventures, feedback on early ideas, and time to meet others in the field.

About the program

About

What this is

Medical Entrepreneurship & Innovation is based at UMass Chan Medical School and open to anyone in Massachusetts interested in how medicine and technology come together: students, residents, physicians, faculty, engineers, and founders.

Over a term we meet in the evenings, in Boston, Cambridge, and Worcester. Each session pairs a speaker with small-group work on attendees' own ideas. You do not need a business background, or even an idea; curiosity is enough.
