Medical students meet the healthcare innovation ecosystem.

An Optional Enrichment Elective at UMass Chan for students curious about biotech, devices, digital health, venture funding, and policy. Six evenings connect you with founders, investors, operators, and peers across Worcester and Boston/Cambridge. Bring a question, an early idea, or just curiosity.

A low-pressure way for medical students to understand how clinical ideas move beyond the bedside.

Most of what happens after a clinical observation, how it becomes a device, a diagnostic, a study, or a change in policy, is invisible from inside the wards. These evenings make that part a little more legible. Most students will not become founders, and that is fine; these evenings are about making that world easier to enter.

Across the region

Who students meet

See the map

The elective brings students into conversation with physician-founders, investors, operators, and student teams across Worcester and Boston/Cambridge. Over a term, that adds up to a working sense of the region, and a few people worth staying in touch with.

People

Physician-founders and investors

Doctors who have started companies, and the people who fund them, talking candidly about what the path looks like.

Institutions

Regional schools

Student innovation groups we aim to connect with at Harvard Medical School, Boston University, Tufts, and UMass Lowell engineering.

Community

Nucleate and incubators

An introduction to the wider bio-entrepreneurship community, plus the incubators, accelerators, and tech-transfer offices where ideas start.

Student projects

Bring an early idea

About the showcase

Students can bring early project ideas, hear candid feedback, and begin building the network that makes healthcare innovation less opaque. Nothing here is graded on whether it becomes a company. The first showcase runs with the Fall 2026 cohort.

Get involved

Join the first cohort

Open to T.H. Chan School of Medicine students for Fall 2026, capped at 25. Come to five of the six evenings, keep a short reflection, and sketch one business model canvas to earn a certificate of completion. You do not need a startup idea to join. Curiosity is enough.
