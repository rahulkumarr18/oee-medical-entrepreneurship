Most physicians notice problems before anyone else. Few get shown what happens after the observation.

This elective is the part that comes after the idea: how a frustration at the bedside becomes a device, a diagnostic, a company, or a change in policy, and who you have to know to get there. Bring a clinical problem. Leave with people to call.

About the showcase

The elective is not only about listening. Students who want to will be able to bring a clinical observation, sketch a concept, and present it to founders, investors, and peers across the region. The first showcase runs in the Fall 2026 cohort.

Get involved

Join the first cohort

Open to T.H. Chan School of Medicine students for Fall 2026, capped at 25. Come to five of the six evenings, keep a short reflection, and sketch one business model canvas to earn a certificate of completion. The bigger payoff is the room.
