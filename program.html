Fall 2026 cycle

Bring an early medical idea, get real feedback, and pitch it: from a first rough thought to a room of founders and investors.

We are an incubator for medical ideas, based at UMass Chan and open across Massachusetts. Over a fall cycle we run founder dinners, project feedback clinics, Boston meetups, a case competition, and a final pitch night. UMass Chan medical students can join the for-credit OEE elective track; everyone else is welcome at the public events.

Incubator path

From Worcester workshops to a Boston pitch night, plugged into the medical-innovation corridor.

Worcester · UMass Chan September

Orientation and idea intake

Students and community members bring clinical problems, rough ideas, or simple curiosity. We map each idea to the first questions: user, evidence, clinical need, payment, risk, and team.

Office hours for early teams

Case competition and failed-startups night
