Fall 2026 cycle

Speaker dinners, project feedback sessions, a case competition, and a pitch night in Boston.

Over the fall cycle we run speaker dinners, small-group feedback on early ideas, Boston meetups, a case competition, and a final pitch night. UMass Chan medical students can join the for-credit Optional Enrichment Elective (OEE) track; everyone else is welcome at the public events.

Incubator path

From Worcester idea intake to a Boston pitch night.

Worcester · UMass Chan September

Orientation and idea intake

Students and community members bring clinical problems, rough ideas, or simple curiosity. We map each idea to the first questions: user, evidence, clinical need, payment, risk, and team.

Problem findingOne-page business planTeam matching
September and October

Speaker dinners

Short talks over dinner from doctors who started companies, professors, pharma and biotech leaders, people who run medical-device companies, investors, health-policy experts, and people building with AI.

BiotechMedtechDigital healthPolicy
October

Office hours for early teams

Small groups pressure-test the problem, the first customer, the science, the regulatory path, how it would get paid for, and the next practical experiment.

MentorshipClinical validationPrototype logic
November

Case competition

Teams work through a real healthcare venture case: what failed, why, and what it means for their own ideas.

Case competitionFailure analysisStrategy
Boston/Cambridge Late November

Regional meetup

A Boston-area evening with students, professors, founders, engineers, and healthcare clubs from across the region.

Boston medical schoolsBusiness-school clubsUMass Lowell
Boston showcase December

Pitch night

Early project teams present five-minute ideas to a friendly room of faculty, founders, investors, and peers. The goal is useful feedback and introductions, not a perfect pitch.

UMass Chan OEE track

A structured path for medical students.

The incubator includes a UMass Chan Optional Enrichment Elective (OEE) track for students who want a for-credit, certificate path through the events. Sessions are planned; some speakers are confirmed, others are being finalized.
